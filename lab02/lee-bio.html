Yuna Lee's Biography

Personal Information

Hello! My name is Yuna Lee, and I am a junior at Syracuse University majoring in Information Management and Technology. I have already completed a concentration in Network and Cloud Computing, and I am close to finishing my second concentration in Information Security Management. I am passionate about the field of security and hope to build a career in this area after graduation.

Throughout my academic journey, I have achieved several milestones. I have consistently made the Dean’s List and received the Invest in Success Scholarship. Additionally, I earned a Machine Learning Foundations Certificate from Cornell University. My experiences include working as a teaching assistant for IST 344, gaining hands-on customer service skills as a grocery stocker and cashier, and supporting sport events as a student gatecrew ticketing worker. This summer, I’m excited to join EY as a Tech Risk Assurance Intern.

Outside of work and academics, I have several hobbies that keep me engaged and fulfilled. I love reading, especially manga, webtoons, and books on my Kindle. My favorite manga genres are romance and shoujo, although Spy x Family is one of my all-time favorites. I also enjoy watching Korean dramas on Netflix and have a passion for collecting k-pop albums, manga, and Tamagotchis. Currently, my collection includes nearly 50 k-pop albums and five ongoing manga series.
